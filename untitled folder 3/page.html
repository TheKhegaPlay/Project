Billie Eilish

Billie Eilish is an American singer, songwriter, and musician who rose to prominence at a young age. Born on December 18, 2001, in Los Angeles, California, she gained widespread recognition with her debut single "Ocean Eyes" in 2015, which was originally written and produced by her brother Finneas O'Connell. Known for her distinct and introspective style, Eilish has since released several critically acclaimed albums, including "When We All Fall Asleep, Where Do We Go?" (2019) and "Happier Than Ever" (2021). She has garnered numerous accolades, including multiple Grammy Awards, and is admired for her unique fashion sense and willingness to address personal and societal issues through her music.

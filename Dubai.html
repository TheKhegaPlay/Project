Billie - UAE, Dubai

What's included?

  • Ticket to Billy's concert in Dubai.
  • Accommodation in a 5 star hotel for 5 days.
  • Burj Khalifa: Visit the world's tallest building and enjoy breathtaking views of Dubai from the observation decks.
  • Palm Jumeirah: Explore the iconic man-made island shaped like a palm tree, home to luxury resorts, restaurants, and the Aquaventure Waterpark.
  • Dubai Mall: Shop at one of the world's largest malls, featuring high-end brands, entertainment options, and the Dubai Aquarium and Underwater Zoo.
  • Dubai Marina: Stroll along the waterfront promenade, dine at waterfront restaurants, and enjoy a yacht cruise or speedboat tour.
  • Jumeirah Beach: Relax on the sandy shores, swim in the warm waters of the Arabian Gulf, and enjoy water sports activities.
  • Dubai Creek: Take an Abra (traditional boat) ride along the historic waterway, and visit the vibrant Gold and Spice Souks.
  • Transfers from the airport to the hotel and vice versa.
  • Guided assistance or tour agent throughout the tour.
  • Other specified services or amenities mentioned in the tour itinerary.
